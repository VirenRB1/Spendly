Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the expense records you enter, including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide. We do not collect payment information or any financial account credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and enabling you to manage your records. We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app for you.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. We take reasonable technical measures to protect it from unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not share your personal data with third-party services. We may use infrastructure providers (such as hosting or database services) to operate the app, but these providers are contractually prohibited from accessing or using your data for their own purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns promptly and transparently.
